Scenic spot in Singapore

The Merlion

The most famous scenic spot is the Merlion located at One Fullerton. The Merlion is also known as the mascot and symbol of Singapore. It has the head of a lion and the body of a fish. It is the most worth visiting spot in Singapore with both good sight during days and at night.

The Marina Bay Sands Singapore

One of the famous view of Singapore is the Marina Bay Sands Hotel, located at One Fullerton near the Merlion. One Fullerton is the most busiest and signatured area of Singapore. The Marina Bay Sands Singapore is also famous setting of movies. It is famous for its infinity pool.

Welcome to my homepage

This homepage aims to introduce Singapore and give out guides for travelling in Singapore. We will introduce the food and scenic spots for visitors' reference.

Singapore is a country in maritime Southeast Asia. It lies about one degree of latitude (137 kilometres or 85 miles) north of the equator. It is a small country with only 283 square miles and 7 million population.

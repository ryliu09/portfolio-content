Food in Singapore

Bak-kut-teh

Bak-kut-teh is a traditional soup in Singapore. Broil pork rib with mixture of traditional chinese medicine materials and spices. The soup taste spicy with white pepper and has strong flavour of meat. Usually we have it with fried dough stick. The most famous bak-kut-teh restaurant is Song Fa.

Crab dishes

Singapore is a country in the South East Asia and is surrounded by sea. Thus, seafood is very common to appear in Singapore people's daily menu. Among all the seafood, crab is the most famous one. Jumbo Seafood is a must-try restaurant in Singapore. It is very popular among both tourists and local people. It serves different flavours of crab and the spicy crab is the most signatured one.

Besides the famous Jumbo Seafood, the Mellben Seafood is also popular. Comparing to Jumbo, Mellben Seafood has a more preferential price. It is popular among local people. The most signatured crab dish is crab soup served with rice noodles. The soup is delicious and the crab is fresh. The rice noodles served with the soup is full of flavour.
